About Namma Schemes

Empowering Citizens Through Technology & Accessibility

üéØ Our Mission

Namma Schemes is a unified government welfare platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government benefits. Unlike traditional portals like UMANG and National Portal of India that function as information repositories, our mission is to provide an AI-driven personalization layer that transforms how citizens discover and access welfare schemes.

Instead of requiring users to search manually through hundreds of schemes, our intelligent system proactively recommends relevant schemes, predicts eligibility with 92% accuracy, and simplifies complex legal descriptions into easy-to-understand language.
